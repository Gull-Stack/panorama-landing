Riverton Utah Real Estate

Riverton is one of southwest Salt Lake County's most established communities. With tree-lined streets, excellent schools, and a strong sense of community, Riverton has attracted families for decades.

Today, Riverton real estate remains highly desirable, though new construction opportunities are increasingly limited. Most available homes are resales, and those seeking new builds often look to neighboring communities like Herriman.
