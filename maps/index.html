Getting Here

Panorama is easily accessible from major highways.

From Downtown Salt Lake City

  1. Head south on I-15
  2. Take Exit 288 for UT-154 / Bangerter Hwy
  3. Continue on Bangerter Hwy south
  4. Turn right onto Mountain View Corridor
  5. Turn right onto Herriman Highway
  6. Follow signs to Panorama

~25 minutes

From Silicon Slopes (Lehi/Draper)

  1. Head west on UT-92 / Timpanogos Hwy
  2. Continue onto Mountain View Corridor
  3. Head south on Mountain View Corridor
  4. Turn right onto Herriman Highway
  5. Follow signs to Panorama

~15-20 minutes
