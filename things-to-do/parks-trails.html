Nearby Parks & Open Spaces

Herriman and the surrounding area offer abundant parks and natural areas for outdoor recreation.

Butterfield Canyon

5 min from Panorama

Popular hiking and biking destination with canyon trails leading into the Oquirrh Mountains. Seasonal wildflowers and wildlife viewing.

Rose Canyon

10 min from Panorama

Scenic canyon with multi-use trails for hikers, bikers, and equestrians. Great for morning hikes with stunning valley views.

Herriman Hills Trail

8 min from Panorama

Ridge trail with panoramic views of the entire Salt Lake Valley. Popular for sunrise hikes and evening trail runs.

J.L. Sorenson Recreation Center

12 min from Panorama

65-acre park with sports fields, splash pad, skate park, and indoor recreation center. Family destination for all seasons.

Oquirrh Lake (Daybreak)

10 min from Panorama

65-acre lake with beach, kayaking, paddleboarding, and lakeside trails. Swimming and fishing in summer months.

Mountain View Corridor Trail

5 min from Panorama

Paved multi-use path running north-south through the valley. Perfect for long bike rides and connecting to regional trails.

Utah is a mountain biking mecca, and Panorama puts you at the gateway. The Oquirrh Mountains offer intermediate to advanced trails just minutes from home, while world-famous destinations like Park City and Corner Canyon are within easy reach.
