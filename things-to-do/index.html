Living the Utah Lifestyle

Utah is famous for outdoor adventure — and Panorama puts you at the center of it all.

⛷️ World-Class Skiing

Utah's famous "Greatest Snow on Earth" is just 45 minutes away. Park City Mountain, Deer Valley, Brighton, Snowbird, Alta — take your pick. With 15 resorts within 90 minutes, you'll never run out of terrain to explore.

Park City: 45 min  |  Brighton: 40 min  |  Snowbird: 50 min

⛳ Championship Golf

The South Valley is home to some of Utah's finest courses. From the challenging Thanksgiving Point Golf Club to the scenic Stonebridge Golf Club, golfers enjoy year-round play with 300+ days of sunshine.

Stonebridge: 15 min  |  Thanksgiving Point: 20 min  |  South Mountain: 25 min

Day Trip Destinations

Utah is an adventurer's paradise — national parks, lakes, and wilderness all within reach.

Utah's Mighty 5

Five stunning national parks — Zion, Bryce, Arches, Canyonlands, and Capitol Reef — all within a day's drive.

Great Salt Lake

Antelope Island and the iconic salt flats are just 45 minutes north — perfect for sunset photography.

Lakes & Reservoirs

Utah Lake, Jordanelle, and Deer Creek offer boating, fishing, and beach days just 30-45 minutes away.
