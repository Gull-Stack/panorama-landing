The Panorama Story

How a vision for Herriman's last foothills became Utah's most distinctive master-planned community.

The Vision

A View Worth Waiting For

The land that would become Panorama sat along Herriman's western foothills for generations ‚Äî open range with unobstructed views of the Wasatch Mountains and Salt Lake Valley below. As the city grew, this land remained untouched, waiting for a vision worthy of its setting.

When Development Associates Inc (DAI) acquired the property, they faced a choice: maximize density and profit, or create something that would honor the land's natural beauty. They chose the latter.

The result is Panorama ‚Äî a community designed around the view.
