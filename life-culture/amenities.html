All Amenities

Every amenity at Panorama is designed to bring people together and make everyday life extraordinary.

Trail System

Wide paved trails loop through every neighborhood and connect directly to nearby mountain hiking and biking paths. Perfect for morning jogs, evening walks, or biking to the park.

Wheels Park

Designed by American Ramp Company for bikes, scooters, and skateboards ‚Äî plus a dedicated RC car course built into the natural topography. A unique addition for thrill-seekers of all ages.

Dog Park

Open space for off-leash play and socializing, because your four-legged family members deserve the view too. Fenced areas for large and small dogs.

Tot Lot

A dedicated play area for ages 2-4 with whimsical structures, shared swings, and shaded areas for parents to stay close while little ones explore safely.

Gathering Pavilions

Covered pavilions throughout the community for birthday parties, neighborhood barbecues, book clubs, or just escaping the sun while watching the kids play.

Pocket Parks

Small parks scattered throughout each village create gathering spots just steps from your door. Benches, shade trees, and open lawn areas for impromptu picnics.
