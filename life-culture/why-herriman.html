Why Herriman

One of America's fastest-growing cities, where mountain lifestyle meets metropolitan convenience.

Utah's Rising Star

Where Growth Meets Quality of Life

Herriman has transformed from a quiet farming community to one of Utah's most desirable cities ‚Äî and one of the fastest-growing in America. What's driving the growth? A rare combination of mountain access, valley convenience, excellent schools, and a community that still feels like home.

Unlike crowded suburbs closer to Salt Lake, Herriman offers space to breathe. The foothills provide natural separation from the valley floor, with views that stretch from the Wasatch Mountains to the Great Salt Lake. Yet you're still just 25 minutes from downtown Salt Lake City and 15 minutes from the Silicon Slopes tech corridor.
