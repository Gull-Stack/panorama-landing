Utah's Finest Builders

Six of Utah's most respected homebuilders, each bringing decades of craftsmanship and design excellence to Panorama.

Building Excellence in Every Home

At Panorama, we've partnered with builders who share our vision: homes that reflect who you've become and where you're going.

Tri Pointe Homes

One of the nation's premier homebuilders, Tri Pointe brings award-winning design and sustainable building practices to Panorama. Known for thoughtful floor plans that maximize natural light and mountain views.

Single Family Homes from the $500s

  • 2,200 - 4,500 sq ft floor plans
  • Energy-efficient construction
  • Premium finishes included
  • Designer-curated options

Toll Brothers

America's luxury home builder. For over 50 years, Toll Brothers has defined the art of homebuilding with exceptional design, meticulous craftsmanship, and personalization options that make each home uniquely yours.

Single Family Homes from the $600s

  • 2,800 - 5,200 sq ft floor plans
  • Luxury standard features
  • Extensive customization
  • Renowned customer experience

Pulte Homes

Building homes for over 70 years, Pulte is known for Life Tested® designs that evolve with how you live. Their consumer-inspired features deliver practical luxury that makes everyday life better.

Single Family Homes from the $500s

  • 2,000 - 4,000 sq ft floor plans
  • Life Tested® floor plans
  • Smart Home technology
  • Built-in storage solutions

DAI (Development Associates Inc)

The developer behind Panorama, DAI brings its signature modern farmhouse townhomes to the community. These are not ordinary townhomes — they're designed with the same attention to detail and mountain views as our single-family offerings.

Townhomes from the $400s

  • 1,600 - 2,400 sq ft floor plans
  • Modern farmhouse design
  • Low-maintenance living
  • Mountain view positioning

J-Craft Homes

Utah's custom home specialists. J-Craft works closely with homeowners to create truly personalized residences. Their semi-custom and fully custom programs bring your vision to life on Panorama's premium estate lots.

Custom Estates from the $700s

  • 3,500 - 7,000+ sq ft custom designs
  • Full architectural customization
  • Premium lot locations
  • Concierge-level service

Elevation Homes

Modern luxury meets mountain living. Elevation Homes specializes in contemporary designs that maximize views and blend indoor-outdoor living. Their homes feature clean lines, open concepts, and premium materials throughout.

Custom Estates from the $700s

  • 3,200 - 6,500+ sq ft floor plans
  • Contemporary mountain design
  • Indoor-outdoor living focus
  • Floor-to-ceiling windows
